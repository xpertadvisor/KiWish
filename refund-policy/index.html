Our Promise

Return Eligibility & Window

You may request a return within [7 calendar days] of receiving your order, provided that:

Products That Cannot Be Returned

For health, safety, and hygiene reasons, the following cannot be returned or refunded once delivered:

  • Any product that has been opened, used, or has had its seal broken

This does not affect your right to a replacement or refund for damaged, defective, or incorrect items as set out in Sections 4 and 5.

Damaged or Defective Products

If your product arrives damaged or defective, contact us within [48 hours] of delivery with your order number and clear photos of the item and packaging. Once verified, we will arrange a free replacement or a full refund, including any delivery charges paid.
