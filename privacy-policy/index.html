Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data directly from you when you place an order, create an account, sign up for marketing updates, or contact our customer support. We also collect technical data automatically when you browse our website, through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies.

  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorised transactions
  • To comply with our legal, tax, and accounting obligations under Malaysian law
